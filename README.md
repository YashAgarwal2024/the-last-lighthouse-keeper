# 🌊 The Last Lighthouse Keeper — Interactive Story

An immersive **interactive storytelling website** that blends narrative writing, motion design, and web technologies to create a cinematic reading experience.

The project presents a short story titled **"The Last Lighthouse Keeper"**, where animations, illustrations, and scrolling effects bring each chapter to life.

## 📚 Story Concept

*"Some lights are kept not for the ships that still sail, but for the ones that never returned."*

The story follows **Maren**, the last keeper of an ancient lighthouse at **Veilstone Point**.  
When mysterious lights begin appearing beneath the ocean surface, she discovers the sea may be trying to communicate.

The narrative unfolds across multiple animated chapters including:

- Prologue
- The Keeper
- The Signal
- The Descent
- The Answer

Each section is enhanced with animations and visual storytelling.
